This website uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience on our website.

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide a better user experience. Cookies can remember your preferences, track your interactions with the website, and help improve its performance.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to analyze how visitors use our website, so we can measure and improve its performance. They help us understand which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors navigate around the site.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies enhance your experience on the website by remembering your preferences and settings. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we have added to our pages.
